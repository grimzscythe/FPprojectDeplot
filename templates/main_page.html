descripition

Inflation as measured by the consumer price index.

Unemployment refers to the share of the labor force that is without work but available for and seeking employment.

Variable Units

Variable Units

Burden of cardiovascular diseases (general term for conditions affecting the heart or blood vessels, e.g., coronary heart disease, cerebrovascular disease) among population expressed in DALYs per 100,000 people.

Burden of chronic respiratory diseases (diseases that affect the lungs and airways, e.g., asthma, lung cancer) among population expressed in DALYs per 100,000 people.

Burden of enteric infections (caused by micro-organisms such as viruses, bacteria and parasites that cause intestinal illness. These diseases most frequently result from consuming contaminated food or water) among population expressed in DALYs per 100,000 people.

Burden of neoplasms (a type of abnormal and excessive growth of tissue that can lead to cancer) among population expressed in DALYs per 100,000 people.

Burden of tropical diseases (any disease that is indigenous to tropical or subtropical areas of the world or that occurs principally in those areas, e.g., malaria, yellow fever, dengue) among population expressed in DALYs per 100,000 people.

***Definition of Disability-Adjusted Life Years (DALYs) :
  The overall burden of disease is assessed using the disability-adjusted life year (DALY), a time-based measure that combines years of life lost due to premature mortality (YLLs) and years of life lost due to time lived in states of less than full health, or years of healthy life lost due to disability (YLDs). One DALY represents the loss of the equivalent of one year of full health.
